The Process

Your accounts, in your pocket — always one message away

No Xero portal to open. No desktop required. Your finance assistant is available all day inside the Microsoft Teams you already use.

1
Open Teams
The assistant lives inside Microsoft Teams as a bot. No new software, no separate login — just open the chat and start typing.
2
Type Your Request
Write naturally — "Invoice Hargreaves Ltd £2,400, 30-day terms", "What's my cash position?", or "Log an £85 lunch expense." No special syntax needed.
3
AI Understands
The assistant interprets your intent and calls the Xero API securely in the background — detecting language, entities, and action automatically.
4
Action or Report
It either executes the action — invoice raised, expense logged, reminder sent — or delivers the financial data you asked for, with your confirmation when needed.
5
Confirms ✓
You receive a clear confirmation with invoice numbers, amounts, or report data — all without leaving Microsoft Teams.

From raising invoices to chasing overdue payments and pulling real-time financial snapshots — the assistant gives finance teams and business owners immediate access to their Xero data through simple conversation.

Invoicing & Receivables
Raise Invoices and Chase Payments by Chat
  • Raise a sales invoice with a single message — contact, amount, line items, and due date
  • Check the payment status of any invoice by client name or invoice number in Xero
  • View all overdue receivables ranked by age and amount, instantly
  • Send payment reminder emails to one or multiple contacts in one request
  • Query revenue by contact, date range, or account code

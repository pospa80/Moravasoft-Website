The Process

Your accounts, in your pocket — always one message away

No accounting portal to open. No desktop required. Your finance assistant is available all day inside the Microsoft Teams you already use.

1
Open Teams
The assistant lives inside Microsoft Teams as a bot. No new software, no separate login — just open the chat and start typing.
2
Type Your Request
Write naturally — "Create an invoice for 2.400 € for Jansen BV", "What's my cash position?", or "Log a 85 € lunch expense." No special syntax needed.
3
AI Understands
The assistant interprets your intent and calls the Exact Online REST API securely in the background — detecting language, entities, and action automatically.
4
Action or Report
It either executes the action — invoice created, expense logged, reminder sent — or delivers the financial data you asked for, with your confirmation when needed.
5
Confirms ✓
You receive a clear confirmation with invoice numbers, amounts, or report data — all without leaving Microsoft Teams.

What It Does

Your accounting command centre, right inside Teams.

From invoice creation to overdue receivables and real-time financial snapshots — the assistant gives finance teams and business owners immediate access to their Exact Online data through simple conversation.

🧾
Invoicing & Receivables
Create Invoices and Chase Payments by Chat
  • Create a sales invoice with a single message — customer, amount, line items, and due date
  • Check the payment status of any invoice by client name or invoice number
  • View all overdue receivables ranked by age and amount, instantly
  • Send payment reminder emails to one or multiple clients in one request
  • Query revenue by client, date range, or product category
Sample Interaction
💬 "Create invoice for Jansen BV, 2.400 €, 30-day terms" → Done. Invoice #2024-084 created.
💬 "Who owes me the most?" → De Vries & Partners, 6.800 €
💬 "Send reminders to overdue clients" → 3 reminders sent.
💬 "Revenue this month vs last month" → 38.400 € vs 31.200 € (+23%)
📊
Expenses & Financial Reporting
Log Expenses and Pull Reports Instantly
  • Log an expense in seconds: "85 € client lunch, charge to project Bakker"
  • Pull a real-time cash position, P&L snapshot, or balance sheet summary on demand
  • View upcoming payables and flag any due today or this week
  • Query transactions by date, amount, supplier, or GL account
  • Generate a VAT summary or period report with a single request
